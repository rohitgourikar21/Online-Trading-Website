We pioneered the Discount broking model in india.
Now, we are breaking ground with our technology



We kick-started operations on the 1st of june, 2021 with the goal of breaking all barriers that traders and investors face in India in terms of cost, support, and technology. We named the company R & R Securities. Today, our disruptive pricing models and in-house technology have made us the biggest stock broker in India in terms of active retail clients. Over 1+ million clients place millions of orders every day through our powerful ecosystem of investment platforms, contributing over 15% of all Indian retail trading volumes. In addition, we run a number of popular open online educational and community initiatives to empower retail traders and investors. Rainmatter, our fintech fund and incubator, has invested in several fintech startups with the goal of growing the Indian capital markets. And yet, we are always up to something new every day.
